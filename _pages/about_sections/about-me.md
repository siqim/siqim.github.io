I am a final year PhD student in the Machine Learning program at [Georgia Tech](https://www.gatech.edu/)'s School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, supervised by [Prof. Pan Li](https://sites.google.com/view/panli-purdue). Prior to that, I worked at [Tencent](https://www.tencent.com/) Big Data Lab after receiving my master's degree from the [University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign](https://illinois.edu/). My Chinese given name is 思奇(siqi), pronounced as 思/si/ 奇/tʃiː/. My Chinese surname is 苗(miao), pronounced as 苗/mɪaʊ/.

My current research focuses on:
- <strong>Agentic AI</strong>, where I study how LLM agents interact with structured information spaces: retrieving evidence from knowledge graphs, simulating network dynamics, and deriving graph-induced data for post-training search agents.
- <strong>AI for science</strong>, particularly on geometric deep learning applications in high-energy physics.
